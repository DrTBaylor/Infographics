Teaching Notes

Toroidal Adjacency: The K-map wraps both horizontally and vertically. Top row is adjacent to bottom row; left column is adjacent to right column.
Valid Group Sizes: Groups must contain 2n cells: 1, 2, 4, 8, or 16 cells.
Corner Group: The four corners (m0, m2, m8, m10) form a valid group! They simplify to B'D'.
Try these examples:
