This Is a Thought Tool, Not a Calculator

The slider values and composite scores shown here are directional, not prescriptive. They are meant to help you think through the tradeoffs involved in using AI for different creative tasks — not to produce a definitive answer.

The preset values assigned to each use case reflect one person's rough intuition and are intended as conversation starters. Reasonable people will disagree on where the sliders should land, and that's exactly the point.

Different use cases surface different factors — and which factors matter most is itself part of the discussion.
